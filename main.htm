Mysterious Visitors Spotted - Who are they?

August 20, 2016

A spaceship with two astronauts visited earth on August 19, 2016.

They landed at Ithaca NY, 42.4440° N, 76.5019° W.

The visit was unexpected, and the visitors did not speak earth language.

They could not come out of the spacesuit either.

They stayed for only an hour and left. No one have seen them ever since.


It is still unknown where they come from or who they are.

They looked like human beings, but why can't they come out of the sapcesuit?

Are they time travellers? Is the landing only a mistake or are they looking for something?

Nobody knows the answers yet.


The photographer bumped into the vistors when she was walking home from the supermarket.

They then walked together for a while in Ithaca, and she took pictures of them.
